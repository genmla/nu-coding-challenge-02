About Me

When I needed to defend my interests, they were in English Literature and Early Modern conceptions of birth and motherhood. Now, I read a lot of YA fantasy, watch a lot of cartoons, and fret a lot over houseplants. I still enjoy Shakespeare's Antony and Cleopatra, and Hippocratic texts, and metatheatricality. I also enjoy autumn foliage, camping, and playing fiddle.

I have a Bachelor's in English Literature from the University of Texas at Austin and a Master's in the Humanities from the University of Chicago. People have asked over and over again what I am doing with either of those degrees, and man it would be sweet to tell them I am coding.

I am the lead communications specialist in the Enrollment Advising department at Northwestern University School of Professional Studies. In this communication role, I have gained hands-on experience in HTML and CSS. I enrolled in this bootcamp to expand my coding skills, which I endeavor to leverage in my career.
